Speech to Slides

AI-Powered Presentation Background Generator for Google Slides

Transform your speech scripts into stunning visual presentations with AI-generated backgrounds.

Features

AI Speech Analysis

Automatically analyzes your speech text and suggests optimal slide structure with titles and messages.

85+ Design Styles

Choose from 85 design styles across 11 categories including anime, business, art, and more.

Gemini 3 Pro Image

Generate high-quality 16:9 background images using Google's latest Gemini 3 Pro Image API.

Direct Integration

Set generated images directly as slide backgrounds without leaving Google Slides.

How It Works

  1. Open the add-on sidebar in Google Slides
  2. Enter your Gemini API key (stored locally in your browser)
  3. Paste your speech text
  4. Select design styles
  5. Generate prompts and create AI backgrounds
  6. Apply backgrounds to your slides

Requirements

  • Google Account
  • Gemini API key (with Cloud Billing enabled)
  • Google Slides access
