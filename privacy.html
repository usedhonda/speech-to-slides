Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Overview

Speech to Slides ("the Add-on") is a Google Slides add-on that generates AI-powered presentation backgrounds. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

The Add-on collects and processes the following data:

  • Speech/Text Input: Text you enter for generating slide prompts. This data is processed only during your session and is not stored on our servers.
  • API Key: Your Gemini API key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Google Account Information: Basic profile information (email, name) is accessed only for authentication purposes through Google's OAuth system.

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating AI prompts based on your speech text
  • Creating AI-generated background images via Gemini API
  • Setting generated images as slide backgrounds

Third-Party Services

The Add-on uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Your text prompts are sent to Google's Gemini API for image generation. Please refer to Google's AI Terms of Service for their data handling practices.

Data Storage

  • Settings are stored locally in your browser's localStorage
  • No user data is stored on external servers
  • Generated images are stored directly in your Google Slides presentation

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the service (i.e., sending prompts to Gemini API for image generation).

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. All communications with Google's APIs are encrypted using HTTPS.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in localStorage
  • Delete your data by clearing browser storage
  • Revoke the Add-on's access through your Google Account settings

Children's Privacy

This Add-on is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact
